Today we will

Learning objectives

All pupils

Know that sound can be seen as a wave on a screen — and the wave changes when the sound changes.

Most pupils

Record a short sound, see its waveform and play it back using SoundWave Studio.

Some pupils

Begin to describe how they could change a recording to make the wave look and sound different.

play_circle Hook · 5 mins Smartboard

Watch the sound grow

Play your audio clip with the waveform visible. Do not explain yet — let pupils describe what they see.

  • volume_downStart quiet — the wave is small and flat.
  • volume_upBuild louder — the wave grows and spikes.
  • forumAsk: "What did you notice happening to the picture?"
Questioning

"What happened to the wave when the sound got bigger?" · "Why do you think the picture changed?" · "What do you think that picture is showing us?"
