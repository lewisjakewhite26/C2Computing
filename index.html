Can a computer see sound?

microphone

It listens to your voice and sends it to the computer.

data

Information the computer keeps about our sound.

sound wave

The line on the screen that shows us the sound.

digital

Saved on a device, not on paper, not in the air.

volume

How loud or quiet a sound is.

record

When we capture our voice and save it on the device.

playback

When we listen to a saved recording again.
